Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, and student details when you submit forms on our website. This information is used solely for admission inquiries, communication, and improving our services.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to respond to inquiries, process admission applications, send school-related notifications, and improve our website experience. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect some features of the website.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. All form submissions are transmitted securely.

Student Data Protection

Student records and academic data are handled with the utmost confidentiality. Access to student information is restricted to authorized school personnel only, in compliance with applicable data protection regulations.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or wish to request the deletion of your data, please contact us at info@apexschool.edu or call +1 234 567 8900.
